Savings Rate Benchmarks by Age

There’s no perfect number, but simple targets help. Here’s a common range for total savings (retirement + brokerage + HSA):

  • 20s: 10–20% if you can swing it
  • 30s: 15–25%
  • 40s: 20–30%

These aren’t rules; they’re reference points. If you’re paying down high-interest debt or supporting family, rates can vary.
