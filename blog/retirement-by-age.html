How Much Should You Be Saving for Retirement by Age?

By Grady the Owl 🦉

Retirement may feel far away, but the earlier you start saving, the easier the journey. Use these simple benchmarks to check if you’re on track.

Benchmarks by Age

  • 20s: Aim to save at least 1x your annual salary by age 30.
  • 30s: Grow to around 2–3x your salary by age 40.
  • 40s: Target 4–6x your salary by age 50.
  • 50s: Build toward 8–10x your salary by age 60.
  • 60s: Ideally have 10–12x your salary before retirement.

“The best time to start was yesterday. The second-best time is today.”

Tips to Stay on Track

✅ Contribute to your 401(k) or IRA.
✅ Increase contributions when you get raises.
✅ Don’t cash out retirement accounts early.
✅ Use catch-up contributions after age 50.
