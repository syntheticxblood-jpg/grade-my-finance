What Is FIRE?

FIRE stands for Financial Independence, Retire Early. It’s a movement where people save and invest aggressively so they can retire years, or even decades, before the traditional retirement age.

The Core Principles of FIRE

  • Maintain a high savings rate, often 40% or more.
  • Invest in low-cost index funds or other growth assets.
  • Keep expenses low to reach independence faster.

Why People Pursue FIRE

The main appeal is freedom — the ability to live life on your own terms, work only if you want to, and spend time on what matters most.

How to Know If You’re on Track

Most FIRE enthusiasts use a “safe withdrawal rate” (often 4%) to estimate how much they need to retire. For example, if your annual expenses are $40,000, you’d aim for about $1,000,000 saved.
