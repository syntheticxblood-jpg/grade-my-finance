Benchmarks

General guideposts. Everyone’s situation is unique.

  • Emergency fund: 3–6 months of expenses (we target 6).
  • Age curve (investable vs. FI target): ~20% by 30, 35% by 35, 50% by 40, 65% by 45, 80% by 50, 95% by 55, 100% by 60.
  • Savings rate: Aim 20%+ of take-home; more if late start.
  • Mortgage LTV: Under 80% is healthy; lower is better.
  • Non-mortgage debt: Keep under 20% of assets.
